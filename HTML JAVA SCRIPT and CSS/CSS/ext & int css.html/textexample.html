The braid , or berger de brie, is a large breed of dog traditionally used as a herder and guardian of sheep.

Breed History

The braid, which is believed to have origianted in France, has been bred for centuries to herd and to protect sheep. The breed was used by the French Army as sentries, messengers and to search for wounded soldiers because of its fine sense of hearing.
